Welcome to the Pleasant Grove Chamber of Commerce

We are the voice of business in Pleasant Grove and Lindon cities, including surrounding areas . We support retail and service businesses by creating opportunities for their growth and success through networking, education and advocacy. Additionally, we partner with large corporations to promote economic development and a vibrant business environment.
